Moneysocket Wallet and Chat Tutorial
The Goal of this Tutorial:

This tutorial will demonstrate Moneysocket through hands-on experience using applications built upon the protocol libraries. This includes the Moneysocket Chat App, the Costanza Wallet App and the Custodial Provider. By following these steps, you should gain knowledge of how these pieces interoperate through the protocol and should hopefully stoke your imagination for what kinds of apps could be developed in this ecosystem.

Step 1 - A Place For Money

Moneysocket works on an account model for holding an balance assigned authorized to a socket connection. This account with the ability to transact on the Lightning Network must be provided at the "Terminus".

For those interested in getting deep into developing Moneysocket applications, or are a power user that wants to self-custody all their funds, you can run the Terminus application with your Lightning Network node.

For those following this tutorial, we have a custodial solution that is set up for trying out Moneysocket more casually. It does not require you to run and configure your own node, so you can get up and running quickly. Go to the Custodial Portal and register:

Please head the portal's warning you to not leave sats in the custodial accounts that you are not comfortable leaving as a donation to the project. The portal does not verify your email address, but if you leave a real address, we can contact you in the event the accounts are reset.
